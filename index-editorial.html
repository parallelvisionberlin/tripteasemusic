Sex Having Disco

Somewhere between a neon postcard, a forgotten beach club and a machine dreaming in Italian, Sex Having Disco feels like Trip Tease and Alain Vice building their own private mythology of the dancefloor.

Pt. 2 opens the world further with Milano 2098, featuring Lubó, and a Prins Thomas Diskomiks that stretches the fantasy into a longer, more interstellar trip.

Home Calling

Trip Tease’s remix of DJ Seinfeld’s Home Calling sits inside one of the most elegant remix universes of recent electronic music: MIRRORS (REMIXED), released by Ninja Tune.

The remix carries the emotional architecture of Seinfeld’s world but gives it a romantic, late-night pulse: deep, warm, slightly euphoric, with the feeling of walking home after something important happened.

Studio

The Studio

Trip Tease has collected synthesizers for more than two decades. Today his studio in Roma, Mexico City houses around one hundred different synths and vintage machines.

Some Saturdays, the doors open for public jams where up to sixty people create music together at the same time. No ego. Just machines, curiosity and collective energy.

Bio

Carlos Salame

Carlos Salame is Trip Tease: producer, DJ and curator from Mexico City. His music moves between electronic, disco, lo-fi and cinematic textures.

His work appears through labels such as clipp.art, Astrolead Recordings and Ninja Tune remix projects, building spaces where music feels intimate, human and alive.

Collector of synthesizers, host of studio jams and obsessive builder of atmospheres, Trip Tease is both a music project and a world.
