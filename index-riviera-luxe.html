04 / Studio

The Roma synth salon.

A private machine collection that became a social ritual.

La Roma · Mexico City

100 synths, one room, no preciousness.

For nearly twenty years, Carlos Salame has collected synthesizers, drum machines and strange electronic instruments. In his Roma studio, the collection becomes less like a museum and more like a living organism: some Saturdays, up to sixty people gather to jam at the same time.

05 / Bio

Carlos Salame, alias Trip Tease.

Artist, collector, host and architect of a playful analog universe.

Luxury without stiffness. Machines without nostalgia.

Trip Tease is the electronic project of Carlos Salame, a Mexico City based artist whose music blends disco romance, synth obsession, cinematic melancholy and club sensuality.

His work moves naturally between records, visual collaborations and studio rituals. Around him, machines become social objects: things to touch, share, abuse, repair, laugh with and lose time inside. The result is a world that feels elegant but human, vintage but alive, technical but never cold.
