Sex Having Disco

Somewhere between a neon postcard, an empty diner after midnight and a machine dreaming in Italian, Sex Having Disco builds its own private mythology of the dancefloor.

Italo disco, synth wave, cosmic electronics and late-night romance move through the release without turning into nostalgia. It feels more like a future memory: warm basslines, shiny synths and that strange glamour of dancing at the edge of the night.

Pt. 2 opens the world further with Milano 2098, featuring Lubó, and a Prins Thomas Diskomiks that stretches the fantasy into a longer, more interstellar trip.

Home Calling

Trip Tease's remix of DJ Seinfeld's Home Calling sits inside the MIRRORS (REMIXED) universe, released by Ninja Tune.

It does not try to overpower the original. It opens another room inside it: romantic, late-night, warm, slightly euphoric, with the feeling of walking home after something important happened.

Un, Deux, Trois

Some songs do not ask for a music video. They ask for another dimension.

Created with artist Kris Berle, Un, Deux, Trois expands Trip Tease's visual language into dream logic, surreal animation and abstract landscapes. Strange characters become another instrument, extending the emotional world of the music rather than illustrating it.

Studio

La Roma Jams

Trip Tease has collected synthesizers for more than twenty years. Today, his studio in Roma, Mexico City houses around one hundred different synths and vintage machines.

A few Saturdays each month, the doors open for public jams where up to sixty people create music together at the same time. No ego, no fixed script, just machines, curiosity and collective energy.

The sessions have become part of his mythology in La Roma: part studio ritual, part social experiment, part living archive of electronic sound.

Bio

Carlos Salame

Carlos Salame is Trip Tease, a producer, DJ and curator from Mexico City. His music moves between electronic romance, disco memory, lo-fi intimacy and cinematic club textures.

His releases and collaborations span labels such as clipp.art, Astrolead Recordings and Ninja Tune, with remixes and projects that connect him to artists including DJ Seinfeld, Kris Berle and Alain Vice.

Beyond records, Trip Tease is a world of images, machines and rituals: synthesizers, surreal animation, late-night jams and songs that feel like old photographs of the future.
